About Alex

Alex Carter is a staff writer at Auto News Lab covering the decisions everyday drivers face: whether to lease or buy, how to compare transport carriers, and what to expect when moving from a gas-powered car to a hybrid or EV.

Alex focuses on practical guides — mileage caps, service intervals, real ownership costs — rather than manufacturer PR cycles. Every guide is reviewed against the latest lease-offer data and public-domain sources such as NHTSA and FTC consumer advisories before it is published.

Coverage areas

  • Car leasing and finance comparisons
  • Vehicle transport and interstate moves
  • Auto detailing and ceramic coatings
  • Electric and hybrid vehicle ownership
  • Used-car buying and vehicle history
  • Road trip preparation and safety accessories
