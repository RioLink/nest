Cookie Policy

What are cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site function properly, remember user preferences, and improve overall performance and usability.

Cookies may be temporary (session cookies) or stored for a longer period (persistent cookies), depending on their purpose.

How we use cookies

We use cookies to ensure the website works correctly and to improve user experience. This includes remembering preferences, understanding how users interact with content, and maintaining basic site functionality.

Cookies may also be used to:

  • Store consent preferences
  • Analyze site traffic and usage patterns
  • Improve performance and loading speed
  • Enable optional features such as advertising

Types of cookies we use

Essential cookies

Essential cookies are required for the website to function properly. They enable basic features such as navigation, security, and access to certain parts of the site. These cookies do not require user consent.

Analytics cookies

Analytics cookies help us understand how visitors use the website, such as which pages are visited most often and how users navigate through the site. This information is used to improve content and usability.

Advertising cookies

Advertising cookies may be used to display relevant ads or sponsored content. These cookies are only activated after explicit user consent.

They may be set by third-party services such as advertising networks, which may track interactions across different websites.

Third-party cookies

Some cookies may be placed by third-party services integrated into the website, such as analytics providers or advertising platforms.

These third parties may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies. We do not control how these cookies are used.

Managing cookie preferences

When you first visit the site, you are given the option to accept or reject non-essential cookies through the cookie consent banner.

You can update your preferences at any time by opening the cookie settings from the footer of the website.

Most browsers also allow you to control cookies through browser settings, including blocking or deleting cookies.

Impact of disabling cookies

Disabling certain cookies may affect how the website functions. Some features may not work properly, and user preferences may not be saved.

Data protection

We do not use cookies to collect sensitive personal data. Any information collected through cookies is used to improve the site and provide a better user experience.

Updates to this policy

This Cookie Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in technology, regulations, or website functionality.

Any updates will be published on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Cookie Policy, you can contact us through the contact page on this website.

How cookies are used on this site

Cookies are used to keep the site running smoothly, remember your preferences, and understand how visitors interact with the content. This helps us improve the overall experience without collecting unnecessary personal data.

Some features, such as analytics or advertising, may rely on optional cookies. These are only activated if you choose to allow them through the cookie consent banner or settings panel.

You can change or withdraw your consent at any time using the cookie settings available on the site.
